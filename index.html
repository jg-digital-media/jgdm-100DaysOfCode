In my commit for 25th January 2019, I posted that I've completed my reorganisation of my work via GitHub reducing the number of my repositories. All my projects are either backed up on my server, on Amazon S3 or on version control. Most of my repos are now set to private. I may end up switching back to public from time to time. But don't worry, anything I deem fit for public viewing will be available to see via my domain at https://projects.jonniegrieve.co.uk or in this repository.
